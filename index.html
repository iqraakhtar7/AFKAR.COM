ABOUT AFKAR

AFKAR (Research Journal of Islamic Studies)

The Research Journal AFKAR seeks to publish articles that identify, explore, extend and unify Islamic Knowledge pertaining to Quranic Science, Hadith Sciences, Islamic Law, Islamic History, Sufism, Shariah & Law, Social Sciences, Economics, Politics, Islamic Culture and Civilizations, Comparative Religions and other related disciplines. AFKAR welcomes all the research papers having strong potential significance to Islam and Social Sciences.
